Cards

Cards are built with as little markup and styles as possible, but still manage to deliver a ton of control and customization. Built with flexbox, they offer easy alignment and mix well with other Finesse UI components. They have no margin by default, so use spacing utilities as needed. Cards have no fixed width to start, so theyâ€™ll naturally fill the full width of its parent element. This is easily customized with the sizing of your choice.

Basic Card with Text Badge

Below is an example of a basic potrait card with text badge. Add a .card.card-potrait class to turn a div into a card and add .card-img to hold the image and .card-description-action class as child element which can hold all your text and action buttons. Adding .card-badge-container along with .card-img will setup the card to have a badge, then add .card-text-badge next to the img tag will show the badge on your card.
